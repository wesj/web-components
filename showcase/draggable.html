Draggable

Description

A draggable node fires drag callbacks on itself when its dragged. startDrag when dragging starts, onDrag when dragging is in progress, and stopDrag when its done.

The coordinates passed to these callbacks take into account any offset from the top left corner of the node, to where the drag started.
